Q1. Describe three applications for exception processing.

>> Debuggind, Error management, Resource/File Handling

Q2. What happens if you don't do something extra to treat an exception?

>> the program will terminate and display an error message indicating the type of exception and where it occurred. It can make the program crash or produce incorrect results.

Q3. What are your options for recovering from an exception in your script?

>> Display error message, Terminate the program, Ignore the error, Retry, Raise custom exception

Q4. Describe two methods for triggering exceptions in your script.

>> Raise Keyword, Assert Keyword

Q5. Identify two methods for specifying actions to be executed at termination time, regardless of
whether or not an exception exists.

>> try-finally, atexit.register()
